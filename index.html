Crescendo App

The Crescendo App features the ability for musical artists to create their own website. Users can submit images, videos, songs, upcoming shows, and social media links to help engage fans of their music. Fans can also sign up for the artist's mailing list.

Ghost Pavilion App

The Ghost Pavilion App was created as a personal website for my music project. The app allows fans of my music to sign up for a mailing list, see upcoming shows, listen to our music, and watch videos. Fans can also access a number of social media links.

Climate Change App

The Climate Change App was created to display the effects of climate change. Users can enter in a city or click one of the markers within a Google map to show projected max tempuratures within the continental United States.

Music Quiz App

“Do You Know Bands” Quiz App tests an individual’s music knowledge in regards to contemporary artists. The user answers a set of ten questions and the number of questions answered correctly will be assessed a “grade” at the end of the quiz.
